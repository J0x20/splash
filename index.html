We want you be part of our beautiful social experiment. Join us in building a community of coactive solo adults, regardless of their relationship status, who want to expand and diversity their in-person interactions. When you attend our thoughtfully curated events and experiences designed to connect groups of solos with intersecting interests, you’ll be glad you came.

Solo adults often opt out of activities they enjoy due to societal norms that exclude or sideline unaccompanied adults. It’s time to disrupt the stigma of going out alone.

We aim to increase the options for going out solo by matching you with event and experience hosts that accommodate groups of solos.
You will be able to choose from chill casual gatherings, unique culinary and cultural events, sporting and entertainment options, unique travel experiences, cool immersive skills workshops and bootcamps.

We expect to launch late Spring 2017 with a full experience website and mobile app. You are invited to join mixolo as a pre-launch early adopter (EA) and begin this exciting journey with us. We are asking for minimal personal information during our pre-launch beta to guide us on your interests as we curate your experiences.
